Authentication Steps

  1. Click "Start VNC Container" button below
  2. Wait for the VNC container to start (~5 seconds)
  3. Click "Open VNC" to open the VNC interface in a new tab
  4. In the VNC interface, double-click "open-chrome.sh" on the desktop and select execute
  5. Login to Google Photos in the Chrome browser
  6. Do not close anything
  7. Come back here and click "Stop VNC & Save"
